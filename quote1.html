For once you have tasted flight you will walk the earth with your eyes turned skywards, for there you have been and there you will long to return.
-Leonardo da Vinci

This quote was by the famous Italian inventor, artist, and scientist Leonardo DaVinci, who lived from 1452-1519. He conceptualized flying machines and though he was ahead of the technology of his time, he envisoned a world where fyling was possible for humans. When I read this quote it makes me wonder what DaVinci would think of the world we live in now.
