"It is time for parents


to teach young people


early on that in


diversity there is beauty


and there is strength."


-Maya Angelou

This quote is by the Nobel Prize winning poet Maya Angelou. She fought for civil rights and was an incredibly inspiring person. I have always labored under the belief that the things that make us as individuals different make us as a human race stronger and this quote epitomizes that feeling.
